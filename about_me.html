Freelance photographer, filmmaker, graphic designer, web designer based in Montreal, Canada.

From discovering music at a young age with guitar and piano lessons, I developed a passion for art over the years. Through various experiences, travelling and discovering the world also became priorities I needed to integrate into my life. Photography then became a mean for me to combine both of my passions and discover the beauties that make up the world we live in. Now I want to make you discover the world as I see it through my lenses.

Always on the lookout for new opportunities, don't hesitate to contact me via e-mail at adrien.tosti@icloud.com, or by filling the form below.
